Status meaning

Every status is written as text and paired with a border or shape; color is never the only signal.

  • □ pending — waiting at a defined gate
  • ▣ passed — deterministic check completed
  • ○ needs_review — human decision required
  • ✓ approved — human approval recorded
  • × rejected — human rejection recorded
  • ◇ blocked_owner — owner decision required
